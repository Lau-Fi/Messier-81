Known Issues:

- Sometimes a planat may not load its PQS (ground) view. To resolve this you will need to enter it, and then revert flight back to start. If you are doing sandbox mode, I reccomend using cheats to
  position yourself on the planet/moon and then reverting back to load before you actually visit one. This issue is less common on campaign mode.
- Entering Wa'an and Helteya's orbits will most likely kill you, sending you flying towards the star.
